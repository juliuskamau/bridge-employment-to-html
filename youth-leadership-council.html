By FHI 360

What are you really good at? I'm good at dancing and treating people with kindness and respect. What makes you happiest? I am happiest when I receive gifts, as I love gifts. Also long road trips. What helps you stay motivated when you feel sad or if you find something challenging? When faced with a challenge I tell myself that everything in life happens for a reason. I review my goals and my goals motivate me more. I will overcome the challenges I'm facing and I will succeed at the end.
